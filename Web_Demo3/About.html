Natural Travels

We will hold many activities related to nature travel.We hope more and more people can understand and enjoy the fun and beauty of nature through our activities.In addition, we can make people more cherish and cherish the environment around us through natural travel activities, because with the development of human beings, our environment has become worse.Therefore, we will hold different travel activities in different seasons such as capming, climbing, hiking.

Wildlife

As the range of human activity increases, more and more wild animals are on the verge of extinction and even have permanently disappeared on this earth.We hope that through our activities we call on people to protect wildlife better.

In our activities, we will go to the Wildlife Museum to learn about some extinct wildlife.Moreover We will also visit the zoo near the museum.Finally， We will organize the participants to go to Africa to learn about the most primitive wildlife.
